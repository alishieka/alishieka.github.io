Website Planning Assignment

Target Audience Description

The target audience of the website:

Who: All types of visitors

Age: 12 to 100

Technology: All digital users

Income: Various income levels

Motivation: To plan their upcoming activities and for the occasion. To determine weather conditions for outdoor activities.

Personas 1

James Smith

Occupation: Tour Guide at Soda Springs

Demographics: Age: 33. Going to university for a degree in Computer Information Technology with an emphasis in databases

Goals: Wants to make sure that every visitor on the tour has a fun time and is safe with his driving and the weather.

Environment: Between his work and school, he does not have much time. With his major being CIT he knows how to operate a personal computer and websites. He likes webpages to be easy to navigate, especially when those webpages will allow him keep visitors safer.

Quote: "The beauty of simplicity surpasses the need for things."

Scenarios

Scenario 1: James has a tour beginning in about fifteen minutes. He needs to see the weather conditions to allow him to be aware of possible conditions on the trail so he can drive safer and let the tour group know how to prepare for the weather.

Scenario 2: He and some friends are planning to go on a hike trail. To make sure that each person is as safe as possible, James can look at the weather to be aware of the conditions on the trail and prepare for the weather on their hike.

Scenario 3: To save money, he rides his scooter to school. Before he leaves his apartment, he checks the weather to see if he should bring his jacket, or to ride slower or leave earlier in case of ice.

Scenario 4: He and his girlfriend planned a date to go on a picnic dinner and watch a movie outdoors. James needs to check the weather to see if they need to bring jackets or move their movie undercover.

Scenario 5: Winter is approaching and his truck still does not have winter tires. James needs to know which day would be the safest day to take his truck in to get winter tires put on.

Personas 2

Grandma Jenny

Occupation: Retiree

Demographics: Age: 75. Widowed. 8 children. 30 grandchildren.

Goals: Jenny loves to plan family gatherings.

Environment: Jenny took a computer basics class some years ago, but with advances in technology she is behind. Simple websites and applications are a necessity.

Quote: "My cell phone is acting up, I keep pressing the home button but when I look around, I'm still at work."

Scenarios

Scenario 1: Jenny wants "Dinner-At-Grandma's" to be outdoors. She needs to check the weather to see if it will be a comfortable afternoon outdoors.

Scenario 2:For her morning walks, Jenny checks the weather to decide what would be most appropriate to wear in that temperature.

Scenario 3: Jenny is planning a play-day with her grandkids and needs to know what the weather will be like to plan ahead.

Scenario 4: Jenny and her children are planning a 4 day get-together and wants to make the most of the 4 days. She checks the weather to see what the best 4 consecutive days would be to plan their event.

Scenario 5: Jenny's grandchildren want to go skiing, and she wants to take them. She needs to figure out which day would have the safest snow for skiing.
