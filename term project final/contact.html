About Us

Our hotel was founded in 2007 in Bethesda, Maryland. We started with one hotel serving patrons of the Washington, D.C. Temple. Since then, we have expanded to 4 different locations across the United States of America. In maintaining an atmosphere of reverence and peace for all who stay at our hotels, we are committed to giving quality lodgings for temple patrons. Our main headquarters is located in Bethesda, which is less than ten minutes from the Washington, D.C. Temple.

Booking agents are available to speak you 24/7 every week. Our agents are eager to help answer any questions concerning amenities, pricing, and long-term accommodations. We look forward to feedback regarding the quality of our hotels. If you have any issues during your stay, please do not hesitate to reach out.
