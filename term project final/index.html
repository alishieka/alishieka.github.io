Welcome

Temple Inn & Suites has amenities and services made available to temple patrons, temple workers, and their families. We provide unique services to meet the needs of temple patrons, and for those who participate in events like youth trips, weddings, marriage sealings, and receptions. Our hotels reflect the culture of the church, give accessibility and comfort, and support a temple-oriented theme.
