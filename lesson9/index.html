Idaho Met Service

Idaho Met Service is a meteorological organization, established in 2021 and based in Rexburg, Idaho. Operated and owned by meteorologists, Idaho Met Service specializes in forecasting weather for locations around Idaho. Idaho Met Service also gives a variety of weather training courses for the military as well as the public. Idaho Met Service gives specialized forecasts for important weather-dependent activities, like weddings, concerts, or parties.
